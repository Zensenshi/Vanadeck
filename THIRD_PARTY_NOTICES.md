- Project: KenshiDRK/mappy--Kenshi-Version
- Website: https://github.com/KenshiDRK/mappy--Kenshi-Version

Credits in the Mappy/map-pack ecosystem include Brehanin's Map Pack, formerly Sean's Map Pack, JP/NA/EU players who contributed map work, and Windower community `map.ini` contributors.
